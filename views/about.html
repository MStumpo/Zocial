Talk to your dreams, one chat at a time


Zocial is a social network based on remotely making a room where people can interact with avatars and short-lived messages, essentially making a virtual party (well that last part just explained the whole thing).


Basically, imagine the most famous person on Earth is hosting a room. You can join the room, interact with everyone around and get a chance to actually meet and talk to that famous person. And since every user gets their own message space and temporary messages, it fixes the problem of having a big mess and a chat as fast as a dog on Red Bull.

I'm also thinking of adding a file system so people can share their projects and ideas
